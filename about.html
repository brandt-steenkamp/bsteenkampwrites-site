About

My name is Brandt Steenkamp. I write satire, romance, essays, and the occasional technical rant.

Alongside writing, I build and contribute to software projects, mainly focused on accessibility and tools for blind or low-vision users.

This website is my home base on the internet. It hosts my satire, software projects, and eventually my books.

The site is intentionally simple, fast, and accessible. No trackers, no ads, and no unnecessary nonsense.

Writing

My writing ranges from brutal satire to trauma-informed romance.

Satire is where I sharpen the knives. Romance is where I put the heart back together again.

Software

I also work on accessibility-related software projects, particularly tools that make technology easier to use for blind users.

Most of this work is open source and available on GitHub.
